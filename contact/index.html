Lerwill Bowen Architects is a practice founded in 1996 in Milan, and since 2000 based in Pesaro, Italy, that provides architecture, urban, interior, theatre and product design services.

We are a practice that concentrates on a few select projects each year. These projects can vary from product to theatre design, from landscape to interior design and from architecture to development: The latter you can see in our menu section "Made to Measure". The common thread through our work are the rewarding projects for high quality hotels and houses.
